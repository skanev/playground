> Show that $\lceil n / 2^{h+1} \rceil \ge 1 / 2$ for $0 \le h \le \lfloor
> \lg n \rfloor $.

First let's establish:

$$
h \le \lfloor \lg n \rfloor \le \lg n \\\\
\Downarrow \\\\
2^h \le n
$$

And also:

$$
\bigg\lceil \frac{n}{2^{h+1}} \bigg\rceil \ge
  \frac{n}{2^{h+1}} =
  \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{n}{2^h} \ge
  \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{n}{n} =
  \frac{1}{2}
$$
